About Me

Hi! My name is Tyler Brooks, and I am learning to be a Web Developer. I decided to learn how to code after building a "Hackintosh" desktop computer. That was a project I jumped into with zero experience and had so much fun completing! It was amazing to me that you could "trick" hardware into behaving differently than intended simply by changing the software and code it runs on. After that I wanted to learn more about programming and build cool, new projects from scratch.

Besides my interest in computers I have a great love and passion for music. I am an avid listener of a very broad range of genres and love watching live performances. I also play acoustic guitar and sing! I currently have a YouTube channel with some of my favorite song covers as well as original music.
